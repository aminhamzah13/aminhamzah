Professional Journey

My career began in Technical Support for the Jakarta City Government and Wisma Atlet Hospital, where I built a strong foundation in system troubleshooting and user empathy. This experience naturally evolved into Quality Assurance, where I found my passion for preventing issues before they reach the end-user.

Transitioning into the Banking Sector, I served as a Manual QA at Bank Central Asia (BCA), handling critical systems like Merchant Apps and EDC. Currently, I contribute to Bank Mandiri and Bank Raya, validating complex financial modules such as Cash Management and Payroll. I focus on delivering high-precision testing to ensure zero-leakage releases in high-pressure agile environments.
